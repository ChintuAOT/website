Me

I'm Thomas Godden, a hardware engineer with profesional and academic experience in most of the full-stack of robotics (hardware, electronics, firmware and software). I currently do electronics, firmware and a bit of hardware for robots at Pipedream Labs in Austin TX. I studied Design Enginereing at Imperial College London with a masters project on learning for bipedal robot locomotion.

I like to work on complex, high power and/or moonshot problems, particularly ones involving autonomous systems with interesting planning, dynamics and control aspects.
